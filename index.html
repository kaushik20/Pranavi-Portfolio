About Sri Pranavi Chintalapudi

A multifaceted artist and scholar

Sri Pranavi Chintalapudi is an accomplished Kuchipudi dancer, trained under Smt. Preethi Tatambhotla for eight years, and a Computer Science student at BITS Pilani, Dubai Campus.

Starting her Kuchipudi journey at age 5, she has performed lead roles in ballets like Kalyana Srinivasam and Navarasa Natabhamini, earning accolades from renowned artists like Sri Tanikella Bharani garu and Oscar Awardee Sri Chandrabose garu.

A certified Kuchipudi dancer from the University of Silicon Andhra, USA, she is pursuing a Diploma while excelling academically.

A recipient of titles, “Nritya Vismaya” and “Naatya Surabhi”, she performed across India and UAE at various religious and cultural venues such as Nadaneerajanam, Guruvayoor, Chidambaram and Kolhapur.

Pranavi is a winner of several prizes which include Shobha Naidu International Award, initiated in the memory of the Guru of her Guru, and Rajiv Gandhi Pratibha Puraskar Award by the Govt of Telangana in collaboration with Singidi Organization just immediately after 5 days of completing her Kuchipudi Ranga Pravesam

Pranavi is also a trained Carnatic vocalist, with an "Outstanding" remark in her 4th-grade certification from Bridge Academy, Chennai.

An ardent lover of instruments, she earned acclaim on Carnatic Violin.

An aspiring polyglot, she is mastering Sanskrit through Samskrutha Bharathi Institute LLC, Dubai, reflecting her commitment to cultural heritage.
